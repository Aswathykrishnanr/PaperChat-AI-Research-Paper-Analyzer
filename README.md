# 📄 PaperChat — AI Research Assistant

PaperChat is an AI-powered research assistant that lets you 
upload multiple research papers and have a conversation with 
them instantly. No more reading hundreds of pages manually.

##  What It Does

- Upload one or more research papers (PDF)
- Ask questions in natural language
- Get accurate answers pulled directly from your papers
- See exactly which paper the answer came from

## 🧠 How It Works

PaperChat uses **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)** —
a technique that finds the most relevant sections from your 
papers and uses AI to generate accurate, cited answers.

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend** — Streamlit
- **RAG Pipeline** — LangChain
- **Vector Database** — ChromaDB  
- **Embeddings** — Sentence Transformers
- **LLM** — Llama 3
